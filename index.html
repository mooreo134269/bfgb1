Who We Are

About PixelForge Studios

Building worlds, one pixel at a time

Founded in 2018 in Seattle, Washington, PixelForge Studios began as a small team of four passionate game developers with a shared vision: to create games that leave lasting impressions on players worldwide.

Over the years, we've grown into a team of 25 talented individuals, including artists, programmers, designers, and storytellers, all united by our love for interactive entertainment. Our diverse backgrounds bring unique perspectives to every project we undertake.

We specialize in narrative-driven adventures, atmospheric puzzle games, and innovative indie titles that challenge conventional gaming norms. Each game we create is a labor of love, designed to evoke emotion and create memorable experiences.
